A History of the Internet

Explore a timeline of significant events and discoveries in the development of the internet.

Scroll

  • 1973

    Effort to merge networks through creation of internetwork protocol begins.

  • 1974

    Term Internet is coined and Specification of Internet Transmission Control Program is created.

  • 1978

    TCP/IP is created.

  • 1989

    Tim Berners-Lee invents the World Wide Web.

  • 1990

    Tim Berners-Lee develops first web server and browser called WorldWideWeb which will later be renamed Nexus.

  • 1993

    Mosaic, the first browser targeted toward non-technical users is created. The browser is later renamed Netscape. This browser is largly attributed with fueling the internet boom of the 1990s.

  • 2010

    The first tweet from space is made by astronaut TJ Creamer from the International Space Station.
